About the Book

The Long Game is a thought-provoking exploration into the essence of purpose, long-term thinking, discipline, and meaningful success. It serves as a guide for building a life anchored by enduring values rather than fleeting distractions.

The book offers a thoughtful critique of modern education systems, which often unintentionally incentivize students to focus on short-term achievements — chasing grades, passing exams, and collecting certificates — while overlooking the development of long-term vision, independent thinking, and a clear sense of purpose.

Through inspiring and intellectually engaging insights, readers learn how to break free from this short-term mindset and intentionally construct a deeply meaningful life that creates lasting impact.
